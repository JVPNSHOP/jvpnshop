Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about JVPN service.

What is a VPN and how does it work?
A VPN (Virtual Private Network) creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and the internet. It routes your internet traffic through an encrypted tunnel to our servers, hiding your IP address and protecting your data from hackers, ISPs, and other third parties.
Is JVPN really free from logs?
Yes, we have a strict no-logs policy. We do not track, collect, or share your private data. We don't monitor your browsing activities, connection logs, or any other personal information.
How many devices can I use with JVPN?
It depends on your plan. Our Basic plan allows 1 simultaneous connection, Pro allows 5, and Ultimate allows 10 simultaneous connections. You can use JVPN on all your devices - just not all at the same time if you exceed your plan's limit.
Can I use JVPN for streaming services?
Yes, our Pro and Ultimate plans are optimized for streaming. You can access Netflix, Hulu, BBC iPlayer, and other popular streaming platforms from anywhere in the world.
What if I'm not satisfied with the service?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with JVPN, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked.
